Stackdown

You vs. 3 bot rivals — everyone plays solitaire at the same time. All aces go to one shared pot in the middle. Empty your Reserve and drop the most cards before the buzzer.

How to play

Two ways to play a card: tap it to auto-play it — to the pot if it fits, otherwise onto a work pile. If a card fits more than one spot, those spots light up so you can drag it exactly where you want. While you drag, valid spots also light up:
Pot glows gold
— drop it there to score (+1).
A pile glows green
— drop it there to stack.

The pot builds up by suit: A → 2 → 3 … → K. Anyone can play to any pile, and several piles of the same suit can be open at once — race for the slot.
Your work piles build down in alternating colours (red 7 on black 8) to free buried cards. An empty pile is wild — you can move any card onto it.
Stock: tap the deck to flip a fresh card.
Reserve (the badge): empty it for +10; every card left at the buzzer is −2. Your score already includes that.
