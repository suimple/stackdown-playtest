Stackdown

A 3-minute solitaire showdown. Race 3 rivals to stack the most cards into one shared pot.
Match length
Bot pressure

How to play

Goal: get more cards into the shared pot than the bots before the buzzer. Most cards wins.

Playing a card: tap it to auto-play it — to the pot if it fits, otherwise onto a work pile. If it fits more than one spot, those spots light up so you can drag it where you want.
Pot glows gold
— drop a card there to score it.
A pile glows green
— drop it there to stack.

The pot builds up by suit: A → 2 → 3 … → K. Anyone can play to any pile, and several piles of the same suit can be open at once — race for the slot.
Your work piles build down in alternating colours (red 7 on black 8) to free buried cards. An empty pile is wild — you can move any card onto it.
Deck: tap it to flip a fresh card into your waste.
